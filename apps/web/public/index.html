What Moodish is proving

Mood to meal

A user can type “rainy spicy biryani” or “light vegan salad,” and Moodish extracts intent tags, filters dietary constraints, ranks options, and explains the tradeoffs.

Office lunch mode

Teams can plan lunch by headcount, budget per person, cuisine rotation, and dietary rules. The app also models Instamart add-ons for fruit, drinks, snacks, and office extras.

Bring your own AI key

Reviewers can paste an OpenRouter key in Developer view. It is used only for that request, never displayed back, and not stored in recommendation memory.

Safe commerce layer

Moodish prepares a confirmed cart preview but does not place orders. Live Swiggy Food and Instamart MCP calls are isolated behind the gateway.
